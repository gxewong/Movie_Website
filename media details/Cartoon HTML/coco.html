Overview

Coco is a vibrant 2017 Disney/Pixar film about 12-year-old Miguel, a boy who dreams of being a musician despite his family's ban on music, and on the magical Día de los Muertos (Day of the Dead), he's accidentally transported to the colorful Land of the Dead, where he teams up with a trickster spirit, Héctor, to find his deceased musician great-great-grandfather and uncover his family's true history, learning profound lessons about family, memory, and legacy along the way.
