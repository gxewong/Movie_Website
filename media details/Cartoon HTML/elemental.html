Overview

In the movie, we follow the adventures of Ember and Wade, two distinct characters in a unique urban setting inhabited by individuals aligned with the elements of fire, water, earth, and air. Ember, with her passionate disposition, and Wade, who embodies ease and adaptability, embark on a path of discovery, uncovering the surprising similarities they share.
